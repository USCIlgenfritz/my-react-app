What is the World Driver's Championship?

The World Driver's Championship (WDC) is the title awarded to the most successful Formula One driver over a season, based on the points system.

What is the World Constructor's Championship?

The World Constructor's Championship (WCC) is awarded to the most successful Formula One constructor over a season, calculated by accumulating points scored by both of its drivers.

How Does Scoring Work?

Points are awarded to drivers based on their finishing positions in races. The current system awards points to the top ten finishers, with the winner receiving 25 points. Second place will get 18 points and third getting 15 points.
