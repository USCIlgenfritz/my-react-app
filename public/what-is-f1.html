What is Formula One?

Formula One (F1) is the pinnacle of international auto racing, featuring cutting-edge technology and some of the fastest cars in the world. The F1 World Championship, organized by the FIA, has been a prominent fixture in motorsport since 1950, captivating fans with its blend of speed, strategy, and excitement.

What are the WDC & WCC Championships?

The World Drivers' Championship (WDC) and World Constructors' Championship (WCC) are the two primary titles in Formula One. The WDC honors individual drivers, while the WCC celebrates the performance of teams across the season, making both championships highly coveted in the racing world.
