Formula One

Formula One (F1) is the pinnacle of international auto racing, featuring cutting-edge technology and some of the fastest cars in the world reaching upwards of over 200 MPH. The F1 World Championship, organized by the FIA, has been the highest challenge in motorsports since 1950, stunning fans with its blend of speed, strategy, and excitement.

Upcoming Races

US Grand Prix

Date: October 20, 2024

Round 19 of the WDC takes place in Austin, Texas. The Circuit of the Americas will feature 56 laps of action around the track. 2 DRS zones and a downhill straight will definitely lead to some great racing. Don't forget to watch the US Grand Prix on OCT 20 at 3pm EST.

Mexico Grand Prix

Date: October 27, 2024

Round 20 of the WDC takes place in Mexico City, Mexico. The Mexico Grand Prix will feature 71 laps of action around the track. This race will be Red Bull Driver, Sergio Perez's home race. Don't forget to watch the Mexico Grand Prix on OCT 27 at 4pm EST.

Brazilian Grand Prix

Date: November 3, 2024

Round 21 of the WDC takes place in Sau Paulo, Brazil. The race will be 71 laps and includes 15 corners and quite a few chicanes. Don't forget to watch the Brazilian Grand Prix on NOV 3 at 12pm EST.
